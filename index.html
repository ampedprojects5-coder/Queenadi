Our Story

About Queenadi Medical

We are a medical devices distributor specializing in orthopedic implants, serving both private and public hospitals across South Africa.

Queenadi Medical (Pty) Ltd was established in 2019 and is owned by Ms. Emily Masango, a passionate and experienced professional in orthopedic trauma implants. With our motto "Fixing broken bones is our priority," we import and supply high-quality orthopedic implants to help address the escalating burden of trauma cases in South African healthcare.

Our team—from head office and warehouse to drivers and field representatives—is dedicated to supporting orthopedic surgeons around the clock. We value feedback from every client and use it to improve our services, training, and the quality of care we deliver.
